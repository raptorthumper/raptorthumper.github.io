Paddys Swamp Trail Bike Visitor Area

Is located only 20 mins drive and approximately 16.8km South of Winchelsea, on Paddys Swamp Road. Paddys Swamp Trail Bike Visitor Area is easily accessed via sealed and gravel roads from Winchelsea and is central to a large network of forest roads and tracks suitable for trail bike riding. 
The visitor area ensures motorcycle riders have quick and easy access to Anglesea Heath forest trails and 4wd tracks. The Trail Bike Visitor Area provides riders with information on where to ride and has a range of facilities to make either end of a ride more enjoyable.

Getting there - The Great Otway National Park and Otway Forest Park are situated south of the Princes Hwy which runs between Geelong and Warrnambool. The Paddys Swamp Trail Bike Visitor Area is easily accessed via Winchelsea, which is situated halfway
between Geelong and Colac on the Princes Highway.

Facilities provided include

-A public toilet
-Undercover eating area
-BBQ's and picnic tables
-Adequate parking for cars and trailers
